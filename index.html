Application

  1. Apply small dot of glue to indiviual lash, let dry for 15 seconds, apply to lash line.
  2. Apply thin layer of glue to full lash line and let dry for 30 seconds
  3. Start lash from where your inner lashes start
  4. Apply lash to the top of your natural lashes along your eyelid
  5. Use tweezers to gently press the falsies against your natural lashes while the glue dries
  6. Coat with mascara to blend natural and false lashes together

Tips & Tricks

  • Do your eyeliner first.
  • Use a lash applicator
  • Trim lashes to match your eyes
  • Always let the glue dry a bit so it's tacky instead of slimy
  • Look down into mirror
